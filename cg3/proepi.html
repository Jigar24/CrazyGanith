Elimination round
  1. 35 questions
  2. Max Time :1 Hour
  3. Your question will be skipped if you have not selected any option.
  4. +5 points for every CORRECT answer
  5. -2 points for every INCORRECT answer
  6. 0 points for skipping a question
  7. Participants can not go back to any previous questions.

 
  Final round
  1. The top 100 participants only enter the Final Round
  2. 30 questions
  3. Max Time :1 Hour
  4. +6 points for every CORRECT answer
  5. -3 points for every INCORRECT answer
  6. Participants can not go back to any previous questions.
  7. Questions after Q.30 will be bonus questions
  8. +5 points for every bonus question answered correctly. No negeative marking for answering them incorrectly
  9. Max 5 bonus questions
 

 
In case of controversy,decision of the Co-ordinator will be considered Final
